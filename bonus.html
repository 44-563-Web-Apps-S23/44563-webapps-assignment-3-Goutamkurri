Green KingFisher

Green Kingfisher is a symbol of hope and protection to those who live near the waterways he patrols. He is always on the lookout for threats to the local ecosystem and will stop at nothing to keep it safe. He is known for his bravery, quick thinking, and unwavering determination.

Super powers of Green KingFisher are-

  1. Ability to fly at high speeds
  2. Exceptional Eyesight
  3. Eye laser beams
